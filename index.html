About IHS

The Indonesian Heritage Society (“IHS”) is a non-profit organization, established in 1970 as Ganesha volunteers, and changrd it's name to Indonesian Heritage Society at 1994

Our organisation provides volunteer support and assistance to Indonesia's cultural institutions promoting Indonesia's rich cultural heritage.

From the initial group of 17 volunteers, today the organisation has grown to include hundreds of individuals. Our volunteers form the heart of IHS, organizing all the events and managing the daily operations of the organisation.
